Building Computers

Old Reliable: Mjolnir

A seemingly daunting task...

Building a custom computer is actually a simple task with the right know-how. And you can get that know-how right here. Computers are modular systems with several primary components. Because the individual components are already designed and manufactured, you don't have to bother with making your own processor, for example. Thus, building a home-built computer is actually a task of putting together parts that are already made for you.

Why build it yourself?

There are several reasons to build your computer yourself. Modularity, price, performance, knowledge, personalization, and really cool lighting are all perfectly good reasons to buy the parts to build your own computer.

Take your cake and eat it too

Building computers is a fun, educational way to save money. It's really a simple process. pcpartpicker.com is a great resource to start designing the blueprints to your own computer! If you don't need a computer, just designing them is a fun and educational process.
